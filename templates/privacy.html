Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

1. Data We Collect

We collect information you provide directly, including your name and email address when you create an account. We also collect expense data you enter, such as amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to provide and improve the Spendly service. This includes displaying your expense summaries, generating category breakdowns, and enabling you to track your spending over time.

3. Data Storage

Your personal data and expense records are stored securely on our servers. You have the right to request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us.

4. Third Party Services

We do not sell, trade, or transfer your personal information to outside parties. We may share data only when required by law or to protect our rights.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at privacy@spendly.app.
